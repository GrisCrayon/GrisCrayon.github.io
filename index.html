What is Graphic Facilitation?

A powerful way to visualize and enhance collaborative thinking.

Graphic facilitation is a visual practice that combines drawing and text to map out ideas, concepts, and discussions in real-time. It helps teams and individuals see the big picture, fostering understanding and innovation through collaborative visual storytelling.

This method is often used in workshops, meetings, and conferences to make ideas tangible and memorable. Graphic facilitation bridges the gap between abstract concepts and concrete understanding, engaging participants in the process.

Want to learn more? Check out the Wikipedia page on Graphic Facilitation.
